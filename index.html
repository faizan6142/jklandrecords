📖 How to Use This Portal

1

View Land Records

Select your district, tehsil, and village to access Jamabandi records. All records are digitized and free.

2

Submit Grievance

Found an error? Fill out the grievance form with details and supporting documents. You'll receive a tracking number.

3

Track Status

Use your tracking number to check grievance status. Receive updates at each stage: Submitted → Under Review → Resolved.

⚠️ Important Information

  • Typical Resolution Time: Most grievances are resolved within 15-30 working days
  • Required Documents: Keep Khasra number, survey number, or plot details ready when submitting
  • Tracking Number: Save your tracking number immediately after submission
  • Valid Mobile & Email: Provide correct contact details to receive updates
  • Office Visits: For complex cases, you may be asked to visit the Tehsil office
  • Service Hours: Revenue offices operate 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Mon–Fri)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any fee to view land records?

No, viewing Jamabandi records online is completely free. No registration or payment is required.

How long does it take to resolve a grievance?

Most grievances are resolved within 15–30 working days. Complex cases may take longer. You can track status using your tracking number.

What documents do I need to submit a grievance?

Typically: ID proof, land ownership documents (if available), and any supporting evidence. Maximum file size: 5 MB per document.

Can I submit multiple grievances?

Yes, you can submit multiple grievances. Each will receive a unique tracking number. Submit separate applications for different issues.

What if I lost my tracking number?

You can search using your registered mobile number or contact the Revenue Department helpline at 0191-2345678 with your name, mobile number, and approximate submission date.

Are the land records legally valid?

Yes, all records are official and maintained by the Revenue Department. For legal proceedings, you may need certified copies from Tehsil offices.
